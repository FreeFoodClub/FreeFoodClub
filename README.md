## Hi there 👋
Free Food Club is an Open Sourced Project designed to give Free Food to all our Campus Members, by partnering with nearby F&B businesses both on-campus near the west dorm complex.
It has several main objectives 

We partner with Grocery stores, Resturants, online food specialty sites, anyplace that has food! (Statistically there's an exponentially increasing variety of locations not core-food related).
What you do:
1.Sign up
2.Get text whenever theres food
3.Reserve your spot
4.Pick up your food free of charge
5.New members can join by referral only
